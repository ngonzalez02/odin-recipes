Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon

SALMON

SAUCE

    3 tbsp butter 2 tsp olive olive oil 6 cloves garlic minced 1/2 cup Honey tbsp water 3 tbsp soy sauce 3 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp siracha sauce 2 tbsp lemon juice
