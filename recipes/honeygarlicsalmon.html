Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon


Ingredients

SALMON

    4 (6 oz) salmon fillets 1/2 tsp kosher salt 1/2 tsp black pepper 1/2 tsp smoked paprika 1/4 tsp blackening seasoning

SAUCE

  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp olive olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/2 cup Honey tbsp water
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp siracha sauce
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Pat salmon dry, then season with what is listed above. Set aside. Adjust oven rack to middle position, then preheat broiler.
  2. Add butter and oil to a large, oven0safe skillet over MED-HIGH heat. Once butter is melted, add listed ingredients above. THen cook for 30 seconds, until sauce is heated through.
  3. Add salmon, skin side down and cook for 3 minutes. While salmon cooks, baste frequently with sauce from the pan by spooning it over the top of the salmon.
  4. Broil salmon for 5-6 minutes, basting with sauce once during the broil, until salmon is caramelized and cooked to desired doneness.
  5. Garnish with minced parsley if desired.
